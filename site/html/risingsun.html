What is Rising Sun?

Rising Sun Esports is a mobile esports organization founded by me and two others. Our team mainly fight in one the most popular mobile game Clash Royale and Brawl Stars. This gave me a great opportunity to dive into the esports community, and I was able to create many graphics for our team and other esports related events.
