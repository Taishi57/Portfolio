About Me


I am a 2D Designer and Visual Artist aiming to create inspiring pieces.
My goal is to create User-friendly designs for all people to have the Most Pleasant Experience Possible.
My strength is that I can Adapt to a variety of mediums, not just specialize in one art style.
I like to try out many things to Discover my strengths and weaknesses so that I can understand more about myself.
I Enjoy looking at my results, whether the art turned out well or not, and continue to Grow from these experiences.
