About Me

I am a 2D designer and visual artist aiming to achieve the ability to create inspiring pieces. I believe artworks should not only be visually amusing, but also turn out to be useful or interactive with the viewer. For example, a sign designed for a public place should be able to be easily understandable by anyone so that they can quickly find the information they are looking for. A designer takes on a very important role when creating these signs because they have to think about the size, alignment, use images/icons etc. My goal as a 2D designer is to be able to create an easy and user-friendly design for all people to see, including signs, websites, and possibly also 3D modeling. My strength is that I can adapt to a varity of mediums and not just specialize in one art style. I like to try out many things to discover my strengths and weaknesses so that I can understand more about myself. I enjoy looking at my results, whether the art turned out well or not, especially when I do something I've never done before.
